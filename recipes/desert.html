Vanilla Puding

This is an easy and very nice tasting desert. It goes well after a heavy meal or when you want something slightly sweet.

Servings: 5

Yield:5 to 6 servings

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 2½ cups milk
  • 1½ teaspoons vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a saucepan, combine the sugar, corn starch and salt. Add milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture thickens. Add vanilla and continue to cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Pour into individual molds rinsed with cold water; chill until firm and unmold.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 135 calories; protein 4g; carbonhydrat 23.6g; fat 2.4g; cholesterol 9.8mg; sodium 166.8mg.
